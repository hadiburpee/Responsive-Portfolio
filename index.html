About Me

I was born in 1982 in Pasadena, California. I am the second of five brothers. I grew up in Glendale playing various sports through the end of high school.

I attended UC Riverside where I majored in Economics. After college, I worked in various industries, and finally settled in the niche area of tax called Indirect Taxes. I am currently a Tax Manager at VCA Animal Hospitals, where I oversee the indirect tax function.

Recreationally, I enjoy the usual, electronic music, hiking, videogames, going out, et cetera, and I've also produced music for hobby on my computer for the past 14 years.
